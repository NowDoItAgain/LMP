Introduction

Explanation of Membership & Retail

Membership

24% off all purchases

  • 24 percent off retail pricing on all products.
  • Members may become Independent Distributors and earn commissions
  • Qualify to attend exclusive Young Living events around the World
  • Members are eligible for the Essential Rewards program, where they can earn up to 25% back, to use for future purchases
  • Membership requires the purchase of a Starter Kit.

Retail

  • Pay full price for all products
  • No opportunity for commissions or the Essential Rewards Program.
  • Retail customers do not need to purchase a Starter Kit.

Memberships are great for both consumers and business-builders (Distributors). Members have the option of becoming distributors and earning compensation or simply enjoying the discounts and exclusives offered.

In the following sections you will learn the advantages of membership for both the consumer and distributor sides. This will hopefully aid in a more informed choice for you.

Members receive a 24% discount on all products. To keep the discount active, each member must buy 50 PV* PER-YEAR.
*PV is Personal/Point Volume, a point value is assigned to each product. Essential oils are 1 point for $1 dollar, but some other products are less.

Membership Requirements

The only requirement for a new member is to purchase a Starter Kit.

The Premium Starter Kit is recommended as it comes with a collection of the 11 most commonly used oils and a diffuser. A perfect introduction for using essential oils in your daily life!

There is NO penalty for dropping the membership program.
